What Demolight does for your dealership

Demolight is the only centralized platform for managing and protecting all your test drive information.

WE MAKE TEST DRIVING BETTER

In today's day and age customers are looking for ways to speed up their buying process and dealers are looking for ways to sell more cars.  No more waisting time coping drivers licenses, insurance cards or writing up test drives.  From our mobile app dealers are able to collect all the necessary test drive information easier and faster than before.  

SECURE ALL YOUR CUSTOMER INFORMATION

By leveraging the use of bank level tokenization technology you can collect all your necessary driver information in a secure environment.  Additionaly, our easy to use mobile app will negate the potential for breaching your customers information by eliminating the use of paper test drives forms.  Thus, reducing your potential exposure to state audits and fines.

MONITOR ALL YOUR DEMOS TRAFFIC

As a sales manager you have many day to day duties that disengage you from your stores demo traffic.  While at your desk, you will easily be able to manager all sales personnel demos.  View the customer driver's license, insurance card, driven vehicle and signatures from your our dashboard.

COMING SOON: MORE TOOLS AND FEATURES

The Demolight team is hard at work on new tools and features to help your business even more.  In the near future you will be able to log your demos directly to your CRM in real-time, search customers by drivers license number and much more...SO STAY TUNED.

Frequently Asked Questions

What mobile platforms are supported?

Demolight currently supports iOS and Android mobile apps for all devices.  We're currenlty working on additional products to support how you sell cars.

How is the data protected?

We utilize secure-by-design cloud infrastructures with built-in protections and global networks used to store your information. What this means is we take a proactive approach to insuring all your customer data is safeguarded using todays most secure technologies.

Where is the data stored?

All data is stored in multiple redundant cloud data centers around the world to keep our service running 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Whats at risk if our data is breached?

From your Demo Dashboard you will have the ability to print a copy of the drivers license and insurance card of each of your drivers.  This demo form will specify your client(s) details and which vehicle you drove, reducing the need to store paper copies.

Whats? CRM and DMS integration

We're currently developing an integration method for submitting any of your test drive details to your CRM or DMS.  Our platform will then accelerate the time it takes to accurately submit your deals.

Whats coming? Analytics and reporting

We understand the truth of your business is in the data.  In the second half of the year you will be able to gather customer data and analytics to understand who your buyers are.
